Student Representation

Why these roles matter

Every role supports a different part of student life. Some roles focus on leadership and policy, some coordinate communication, some support academic growth, and others help improve student welfare.

Understanding the responsibility behind each title helps students vote based on purpose, not only popularity.

“A strong election is not only about who wins. It is about choosing the right people for the right responsibilities.”
