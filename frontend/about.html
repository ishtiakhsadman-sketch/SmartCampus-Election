Election Overview

A smarter way to organize campus elections

SmartCampus Election is a digital election management platform created for student leadership selection. It presents election positions, candidate profiles, notices, schedules, voting flow, and result information in one clean and accessible place.

The system helps students move from information to action: first understanding the election, then exploring candidates, then casting and reviewing their ballot before submission.

Process

How the Election Works

The election follows a step-by-step process from official announcement to final result publication.

01
Start

Election Notice

Official election information and key dates are announced for students.

02
Candidate Entry

Candidate Nomination

Interested students submit nomination information for available positions.

03
Review

Candidate Verification

Candidate information is checked before publishing the official candidate list.

04
Campaign

Campaign Period

Candidates share their goals, manifesto, and campaign messages with students.

05
Voting

Voting Period

Students cast their vote using a simple ballot, review, and final confirmation flow.

06
Final

Result Publication

Election results are published after review and approval by the election authority.
